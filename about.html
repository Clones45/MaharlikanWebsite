About Maharlika

Maharlikan AssuredLife is built on a simple promise:

“Layunin ng Maharlikan AssuredLife ay ASSURED ka!” We are a Filipino assurance company committed to providing peace of mind and dignity for every family. Our mission is to ensure that when life’s most difficult moments arrive, our planholders and their loved ones are protected, guided, and supported with compassion and respect.

Grounded in Filipino values of malasakit, kalinga, and bayanihan, Maharlikan AssuredLife offers reliable assurance plans that are both affordable and meaningful. More than just financial protection, we provide families with the confidence that their future is secured and their memories honored.

Being ASSURED means:

  • Affordable plans for every Filipino family
  • Security and peace of mind when it matters most
  • Support with compassion and integrity
  • Unity in service, reflecting true Filipino values
  • Respect for every life and legacy
  • Empowerment through transparent and dependable care
  • Dignity in every step of the journey

Maharlikan AssuredLife — because every Filipino deserves to be ASSURED.
